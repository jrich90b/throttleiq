## 2. Services

LeadRider will provide the Dealer access to the LeadRider platform for dealership lead communication and follow-up operations.

Included services may include:

- Lead inbox and customer conversation management.
- Web lead / ADF intake.
- SMS and email draft workflow.
- AI-assisted suggested replies.
- Follow-up cadence support.
- Appointment and outcome tracking.
- Google Calendar integration.
- CRM logging support where configured.
- Inventory-aware lead context.
- Campaign Studio tools where enabled.
- Operational reporting, feedback review, and issue tracking.
- Basic hosting, monitoring, and production support.

LeadRider may improve, modify, or replace platform features over time as long as the core service remains substantially available.

## 3. Suggest Mode and Human Review

Unless otherwise agreed in writing, the platform will operate in **Suggest Mode**.

In Suggest Mode:

- LeadRider may generate draft SMS or email replies.
- Dealer staff must review, edit if needed, and manually approve messages before sending.
- Dealer is responsible for the final content sent to customers.
- LeadRider is not responsible for claims, promises, pricing, availability statements, financing representations, or other customer-facing commitments made by Dealer staff.

Any move from Suggest Mode to automated sending must be separately approved in writing.

## 4. Dealer Responsibilities

Dealer is responsible for:

- Providing accurate dealership profile information, hours, staff names, phone numbers, website links, inventory feeds, and CRM access.
- Maintaining proper customer consent for calls, SMS, MMS, and email.
- Ensuring website lead/contact forms include appropriate consent language and links to privacy/terms pages.
- Reviewing AI-assisted draft messages before sending.
- Honoring customer opt-out requests.
- Ensuring sales, finance, service, parts, apparel, and management staff use the platform appropriately.
- Not uploading purchased, rented, scraped, or non-consented marketing lists.
- Providing timely access to required vendor systems, including but not limited to Google, Twilio, SendGrid, Meta, CRM systems, and website providers.

## 5. SMS, MMS, Email, and TCPA Compliance

Dealer acknowledges that customer communication laws may apply, including TCPA, CAN-SPAM, carrier messaging rules, privacy laws, and state-specific requirements.

Dealer is responsible for:

- Customer consent and opt-in language.
- Confirming that contacts are eligible to receive SMS, MMS, calls, and email.
- Using mass messaging only for properly opted-in recipients.
- Not sending prohibited, misleading, or unlawful content.
- Honoring `STOP`, `HELP`, unsubscribe, and similar requests.

LeadRider may pause or block messaging activity that appears risky, abusive, non-compliant, unusually high-volume, or likely to harm deliverability.

## 7. Included Usage and Fair Use

The monthly platform fee includes ordinary vendor usage required for normal dealership lead follow-up, including:

- AI-assisted drafting and classification.
- Ordinary one-to-one SMS follow-up.
- Ordinary email delivery.
- Platform hosting.
- Operational monitoring.
- Basic support.

Included usage is intended for normal sales, service, parts, apparel, appointment, finance, and customer follow-up workflows.

## 8. Mass SMS and Campaign Messaging

Mass SMS/MMS and campaign messaging are separate from normal one-to-one lead follow-up.

Starter Plan includes:

- `1,000` mass SMS segments per month.
- MMS campaigns are not included unless separately approved.

Overages:

- SMS overage: `$0.05` per additional SMS segment.
- MMS overage: `$0.20` per additional MMS message.

Large campaigns, unusually high-volume sends, image-heavy campaigns, or sends to old/stale lists may require prior approval.

Dealer may not use LeadRider for purchased lists, scraped lists, non-consented contacts, or messages that violate carrier rules.

## 9. Third-Party Vendor Costs

The monthly fee includes ordinary usage of vendors used by LeadRider to provide the service, such as OpenAI, Twilio, SendGrid, Vercel, hosting, monitoring, and similar tools.

LeadRider may charge separately or require plan changes for:

- High-volume marketing blasts.
- High-volume SMS/MMS usage.
- High-volume email marketing.
- Heavy AI image/video generation.
- Additional phone numbers.
- Custom domains, dedicated infrastructure, or dedicated vendor accounts.
- Paid advertising spend.
- Dealer website work.
- Custom CRM or DMS integrations.
- Vendor fees outside normal lead follow-up.
